Why quitfacebook.org?

I hate advertising and marketing, and I hate facebook; quitfacebook.org is a means to make that clear. If something grows so large that it can not survive without ads and manipulation, then it should stop growing or cease to exist.
If this site proved useful or made you laugh, consider:
  • buying a quitfacebook.org profile,
  • suggesting new sources,
  • sharing the site,
  • donating.
Better yet, just quit facebook.
